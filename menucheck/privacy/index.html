MenuCheck Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-01

Simple, plain-language policy

1. What MenuCheck stores on your device

MenuCheck saves your Food Profile and food/restriction preferences locally on your device so the app can personalize guidance during assessment.

2. When network services are used

Network processing happens only when you use features that require it. This may include user-triggered photos, menu text, menu URLs, searches, or barcode lookups.

These requests may be processed by MenuCheck app code and/or hosted proxy/AI/menu services. The current hosted backend domain is: foodlens-proxy.onrender.com.

3. Personal data use and sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • The current beta is not designed to include advertising or third-party ad tracking.
  • The current beta is not designed to include behavioral ad targeting.

These statements are based on current app behavior and will be updated if future app behavior changes.

4. Support email and sensitive information

If you contact support, include whatever details you choose. We may receive whatever you send in your support request.

Because support requests can include sensitive context, please do not send highly sensitive personal information unless necessary.

For support or privacy questions, contact longtranpham+support@gmail.com.

5. Safety and medical disclaimer

MenuCheck gives dietary guidance to support your own decisions. It is not a medical, allergy, or clinical recommendation tool.

Please verify ingredient labels and menu details before acting on any result. If you have allergies or medical conditions, are pregnant, are making decisions for children, or follow clinical diets, speak with a qualified professional.

6. Contact and policy changes

If you have privacy questions, use the MenuCheck support page. This page may be updated as product behavior changes.
